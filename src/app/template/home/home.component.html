Vehicle Loan Information

Purpose

  • Purchase of New 4-Wheeler
  • Purchase of Old 4-Wheeler, not older than 3 years
  • Purchase of a New 2-Wheeler
  • Loan for Companies / Firms for purchase of vehicle for usage by their Directors / employees.

Vehicle Loan Eligibility

  • Resident Indian citizen and Non-Resident Indians (NRIs).
  • Minimum age - 18 years and maximum age - 75 years.
  • Individuals may apply singly or jointly with other eligible individuals.
  • Maximum applicants to be restricted to 3 (Main applicant + maximum 2 co-applicants).
  • Companies / Firms for purchase of vehicle for usage by their Directors/employees.

Repayment

  • New 4 Wheeler - 84 months
  • New 2 Wheeler - 36 months (60 Months under Tie-Up)
  • Old 4 Wheeler - 60 months

Moratorium

No moratorium permitted under the scheme.

    • Flat Rs.500 Plus GST for two wheeler
    • Flat Rs.1000 Plus GST for four wheeler

Prepayment Penalty

There is NO prepayment penalty if loan adjusted from own verifiable source.

Security

  • Hypothecation of vehicle purchased out of Bank's finance
  • Bank's lien to be noted with the Road Transport authorities

Guarantee

  • In case of individual: No guarantee required. However, 3rd party guarantee of person having means equivalent to the loan amount, to be obtained, only if the credit bureau score of the applicant/co-applicant is less than 700.
  • In case of NRI, third party guarantee of one/two local Resident Indian, acceptable to the bank, and having means equivalent to the loan amount, to be obtained.
  • In case of companies guarantee of the promoter/director is required.
  • In case of partnership firm, guarantee of all the partners is required.
